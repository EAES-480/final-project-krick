Then, include codebooks (variables, and their descriptions) for your data file(s)
using the following format.

# Mueller_buckthorn_gradient_JAE_2018_Dryad_data.csv

## Dimensions
- **Rows:** 12
- **Columns:** 51

## Codebook

- `plot_id`: identifier for each unique plot
- **`soil_type`: plot soil types, either sandy or silty**
- `slope`: slope of the plot (degrees)
- `aspect`: aspect/compass direction that a slope faces of the plot (degrees)
- `sand_percent`: percent of soil particles that are 'sand' sized
- `total_annual_leaf_litterfall_mass_g_per_m2 `: the mass of leaf litter fall collected during Autumn
- `acer_rubrum_leaf_litter_percent_of_total_litter `:the percentage of total leaf litter fall that is derived from Acer rubrum (red maple) in each plot
- `quercus_spp_leaf_litter_percent_of_total_litter `: the percentage of total leaf litter fall that is derived from Quercus spp. (red and white oak) in each plot
- `other_leaf_litter_percent_of_total `:the percentage of total leaf litter fall that is derived from species other than buckthorn	
- **`buckthorn_stems_per_m2_subplot`: the number of buckthorn stems in each 3 m diameter subplot**
- `buckthorn_basal_area_per_m2_subplot`: the basal area of buckthorn stems in each 3 m diameter subplot
- `buckthorn_basal_area_per_m2_plot`: the basal area of buckthorn stems in each 12 m diameter subplot
- `root_biomass_g_per_m2 `: biomass of roots less than 1 mm in diameter in the upper 15 cm of mineral soil in each plot
- **`earthworm_biomass_g_per_m2`: biomass of earthworms in each subplot**
- `soil_pH `: pH of the upper 20 cm of mineral soil in each plot
- `soil_k_g_per_m2 `: mass of exchangeable potassium in the upper 20 cm of mineral soil in each plot
- `soil_ca_g_per_m2`: mass of exchangeable calcium in the upper 20 cm of mineral soil in each plot
- `soil_p_g_per_m2`: mass of total phosphorus in the upper 20 cm of mineral soil in each plot
- `soil_n_g_per_m2`: mass of total nitrogen in the upper 20 cm of mineral soil in each plot
- `total_annual_leaf_litter_k_g_per_m2`: annual flux of potassium through leaf litter fall in each plot
- `total_annual_leaf_litter_ca_g_per_m2`: annual flux of calcium through leaf litter fall in each plot
- `total_annual_leaf_litter_p_g_per_m2`: annual flux of phosphorus through leaf litter fall in each plot
- `total_annual_leaf_litter_n_g_per_m2`: annual flux of nitrogen through leaf litter fall in each plot
- `total_leaf_litter_k_%`: concentration of potassium in total leaf litter fall from Autumn
- `total_leaf_litter_ca_%`: concentration of calcium in total leaf litterfall from Autumn
- `total_leaf_litter_p_%`: concentration of phosphorus in total leaf litterfall from Autumn
- `total_leaf_litter_n_%`: concentration of nitrogen in total leaf litterfall from Autumn
- `non_buckthorn_leaf_litter_k_%`: weighted average concentration of potassium in leaf litterfall from all species other than buckthorn
- `non_buckthorn_leaf_litter_ca_%`: weighted average concentration of calcium in leaf litterfall from all species other than buckthorn
- `non_buckthorn_leaf_litter_p_%`:weighted average concentration of phosphorus in leaf litterfall from all species other than buckthorn
- `non_buckthorn_leaf_litter_n_%`: weighted average concentration of nitrogen in leaf litterfall from all species other than buckthorn
- `buckthorn_green_leaves_k_mg_per_g`: nutrient concentrations in green leaves or leaf litter of the given species
